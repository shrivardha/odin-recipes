Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth;
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.

Nutrition Facts Per Serving: 345 calories; protein 8.6g; carbohydrates 36.9g; fat 18.5g; cholesterol 70.3mg; sodium 154.6mg.
